MISSION

Built on a chain-agnostic premise, Metafrag aims to keep decentralized metaverses distributed and fair through collective ownership and governance.

If you have wanted to get aboard the virtual real estate train, but are struggling to pool millions to buy digital LAND — Metafrag is the answer.

With Metafrag, you can not only purchase virtual real estate but also break up the land into fragments that can be purchased by investors, laypeople and early adopters alike.

The benefits of virtual real estate ownership will not permeate the mainstream unless barriers to entry are removed, i.e. high entry cost. With Metafrag, owning a parcel of land in a virtual world is made possible for people from all walks of life. Individuals can group up and fragment, buy and sell pieces of metaverse land –- while simultaneously creating a shared digital community.
