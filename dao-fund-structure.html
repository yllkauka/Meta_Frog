STRUCTURE

A successful and sustainable DAO fund will have mostly balanced inflows and outflows of capital.

The DAO fund will hold a substantial portion of the initial MTFRG tokens (about 35% of total supply), and the fund will undergo a vesting period of several years, slowly receiving the platform tokens from a vesting vault. During the first few platform versions, foundation funds will be completely locked while the vesting vault slowly emits MTFRG tokens to the foundation (see roadmap). After a few quarters, tokens that have been emitted thus far can now be voted on for use. The only action that the DAO funds will be used for is to reward early NFT curators at a set capacity.

Users may submit proposals as to what should be done with the funds.

For example, the community might see it fit to purchase a plot of LAND in a popular metaverse to have as an investment and collaboratively govern as a space for the platform users. In this case, the funds would be used to buy a plot of LAND, fragment it, and distribute it for free to all active community members. Another way the funds may be used is to make decisions on successful proposals to add any new features or services.

There is no limit to what the community can do with the foundation funds. They can even decide to donate some of the funds to charity or change how curators are rewarded.
