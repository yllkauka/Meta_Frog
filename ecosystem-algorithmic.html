ALGORITHMIC GOVERNANCE SUITE

Unlike other applications, Metafrag is outfitted with an Algorithmic Governance Suite that gives users access to different metaverses and different chains from the same platform.

Using this tool, you can automate your decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) for each non-fungible token (NFT). Once you purchase an ARC-20 fragment of LAND or other meta assets, your DAO contract will automatically identify your token and give you access to your governance dashboard.

Within your governance suite, you will find the Metafrag platform governance tools where you can submit your proposal to buy LAND assets. Under the suite, you can also vote on DAO fund allocation, decide on platform fees, and augment community rules. This is what makes Metafrag truly democratic and hassle-free to use.
