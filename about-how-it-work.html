HOW IT WORKS

MetaFrag is leading the way in algorithmic governance for shared assets in the metaverse.

As with most investments, the large corporations used to dominate the decentralized markets –- buying huge chunks of LAND in the Metaverse. With Metafrag, it’s possible to break down the same land into parcels of digital real estate that can be sold as non-fungible tokens — with each token having a cartesian value, like 16x16 meters or something else.

An analogy for what Metafrag does comes directly from the physical world. Thanks to Real Estate Investment Groups, many investors have the chance to invest in real estate as a coalition of investors who want to collaborate and cooperatively invest in properties that may have been out of reach for them otherwise, such as office buildings, apartments buildings, malls and more.

Additionally, they may elect to rent out the land, or build high-yield properties and split the monthly revenue according to capital contributed to the land acquisition. On Metafrag, users can do something similar and fragment their LAND and other meta assets into a predetermined amount of fungible tokens, called “Metafragments”, powered by the fragmenting smart contract technology.
