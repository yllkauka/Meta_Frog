MILESTONES

Metafrag v1 - Launches in Quarter 1 of 2022

The native platform will launch with a dashboard interface. In this version, you can fragment your land token and other meta assets and immediately deploy them in your dashboard interface.

Members are also able to participate in collaborative discourse on governance with the help of REIDAO and the discord channels that facilitate.

Metafrag v2 - Launches in Quarter 2 of 2022

This version will allow Metafrag to transform into a marketplace for tokenized LAND assets and accommodate trade activities, such as the sale and purchase of Metafragments.

Within our native marketplace, many options will be listed, giving investors a chance to have their pick after deliberations. At this stage, users will be able to access the algorithmic governance suite tool as well, which will automate DAO contracts deployed and operable for collaborative governance with your meta assets.

Metafrag v2 - Launches in Quarter 2 of 2022

Metafrag will support Solana and Immunable assets. A new feature will allow users to submit proposals within and as a community.

Metafrag v2 - Launches in Quarter 2 of 2022

At this stage, the features would be largely built out and the focus will shift to allowing community funds to be pooled. This would also allow community members to vote on how the funds will be used and distributed.

Users can vote on the new features they would like to see on the platform and we will build the future versions based on what the community wants to see the most.

We will also incorporate assets like Polkadot (DOT) and HIVE at this stage so that our community has more platform chains to vote on.

Metafrag v2 - Launches in Quarter 2 of 2022

One great leap in making digital LAND assets more accessible to all is our new feature that launches in 2023. Using their existing digital land assets as collateral, users are able to take out land loans to buy more assets. The inspiration for this comes from the physical world, wherein people can use existing assets to buy more high-yield assets — we are bringing that opportunity to the digital world.
