MARKETPLACE

Metafrag marketplace functions just like a traditional NFT marketplace. There are listed NFTs for sale, NFT entities, and NFT lands on open-sea that you can buy. There are many meta assets and land that you can choose from. You can also see the price history, the listings and even make an offer.

It is worth noting that although the marketplace lists entire tokens like the ERC-721, Metafrag stands behind the concept of ERC/ARC-20 tokens (which represent the fragmented LAND assets) and a majority of our offerings are in relation to these.

After purchasing a fragment, you can now go to your governance suite for your assets DAO.
